From testing the magnifier, I learned that it was difficult to navigate the page and confirm actions, especially with long traverses causing slight motion sickness. Tabs being spread apart made it harder to see, and finding the back arrow navigation was a challenge. To address these issues, I added some design features to help mitigate these challenges. Firstly, a high contrast mode was introduced, darkening the background and making words white for better readability. Additionally, I made zooming in more user-friendly by implementing wrap-around text and grouping navigation elements closer together for easier access.
